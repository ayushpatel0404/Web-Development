English Grammar Quiz 2

  1. What is the correct form of the verb in the following sentence?

    The dog is barking loudly.

    The dog am barking loudly.

    The dog be barking loudly.

    The dog was barking loudly.

  2. Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

    I have been studying English from two years.

    I have been studying English for two years.

    I have been studying English during two years.

  3. Choose the correct form of the verb in the following sentence:

    The flowers are smells nice.

    The flowers smells nice.

  4. Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

    She has been waiting for the bus since morning.

    She has been waiting for the bus during morning.

    She has been waiting for the bus for morning.

    She has been waiting for the bus from morning.

  5. What is the correct form of the verb in the following sentence?

    I have gone to the store.

    I have go to the store.

  6. Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

    I will meet you on the airport.

    I will meet you at the airport.

    I will meet you by the airport.

  7. Choose the correct form of the verb in the following sentence:

    She don't have any money.

    She doesn't has any money.

  8. Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

    He is more tall than his brother.

    He is taller than his brother.

    He is more taller than his brother.

  9. What is the correct form of the verb in the following sentence?

    They has been working on the project for hours.

    They have been working on the project for hours.

    They has being working on the project for hours.
