English Grammar Quiz

  1. Choose the correct verb form:

    The cat (is, are) playing in the garden.

    is are

  2. Choose the correct sentence:

    He don't like pizza.

    He don't He doesn't

  3. Choose the correct pronoun:

    She invited Sarah and (I, me) to her party.

    me I

  4. Choose the correct preposition:

    The book is (on, in) the table.

    on in

  5. Choose the correct article:

    I saw (a, an) elephant at the zoo.

    an a

  6. Choose the correct past participle:

    She has (eaten, ate) dinner already.

    eaten ate

  7. Choose the correct conjunction:

    I like to read, (but, and) I also enjoy watching movies.

    but and

  8. Choose the correct comparative form:

    This book is (good, better) than that one.

    good better

  9. Choose the correct possessive form:

    The car (belong, belongs) to John.

    belong belongs

  10. Choose the correct modal verb:

    I (can, could) swim when I was five years old.

    can could
